Our Services

Comprehensive medical care for the entire family. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, we're here to support your health journey.

General Check-Ups

Comprehensive health examinations to monitor your well-being and detect potential issues early.

Our general check-ups include:

  • Complete physical examination
  • Blood pressure and vital signs check
  • Blood tests for cholesterol, glucose, and more
  • Health risk assessment
  • Personalized health recommendations

Diagnosis & Treatment

Expert evaluation and treatment of common conditions with personalized care plans.

We diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions including:

  • Respiratory infections (cold, flu, bronchitis)
  • Chronic conditions (hypertension, diabetes, asthma)
  • Skin conditions and rashes
  • Digestive issues
  • Musculoskeletal pain and injuries

Vaccinations

Immunizations for adults and children to protect against preventable diseases.

We offer a comprehensive range of vaccinations:

  • Childhood immunization schedule
  • Flu vaccines (seasonal)
  • Travel vaccinations
  • HPV vaccine
  • Pneumonia and shingles vaccines
  • COVID-19 vaccines and boosters

Health Counseling

Lifestyle guidance and preventive care strategies tailored to your health needs.

Our health counseling services include:

  • Nutrition and weight management
  • Exercise and fitness planning
  • Stress management techniques
  • Smoking cessation support
  • Chronic disease management education
  • Mental health and wellness support

Minor Procedures

In-office minor surgical procedures for convenience and quick recovery.

We perform various minor procedures including:

  • Skin lesion removal
  • Wart treatment and removal
  • Joint injections
  • Minor laceration repair
  • Ingrown toenail treatment
  • Abscess drainage

Prescription Refills

Convenient online prescription refill requests for your ongoing medications.

Our prescription refill service offers:

  • Online refill requests 24/7
  • Automatic reminders when refills are due
  • Direct sending to your preferred pharmacy
  • Tracking of refill status
  • Secure handling of your medication information

When to Seek Emergency Care

Go to the emergency room or call 911 immediately if you experience any of these symptoms:

Chest pain or pressure - Especially if it radiates to your arm, neck, or jaw
Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
Severe abdominal pain
Sudden weakness or numbness - Especially on one side of the body
Sudden vision changes
Confusion or difficulty speaking
Severe bleeding that won't stop
Suicidal or homicidal thoughts
